#FilmClub

An idea for friends

Lizzie Ellis | @lizmeister321

What's the Big Idea?

Bring book club into the 21st century, but with movies. Add a remote component so anyone and everyone can join in.

Let's use the tools we have to hang out more!

The Backstory

The Kicker

Hold down alt and click on any element to zoom in on it using zoom.js. Alt + click anywhere to zoom back out.

The Plan

Movie Selection email goes out on Friday, w/ film links

Two weeks to watch movie

In-person discussion with remote available. Online comment board open simultaneously

Pick new movie as a group online

Wash, rinse, repeat
